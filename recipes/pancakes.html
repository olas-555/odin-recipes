pancakes recipe

description

this is a delicious homemade pancakes with fluffy texture and sweet taste.

ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto the griddle.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
