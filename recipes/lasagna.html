recipe lasagna

description

this is a delicious homemade lasagna with layers of pasta,cheese,and rice tomato sauce.

ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
